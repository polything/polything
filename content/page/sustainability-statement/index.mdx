<p>At <strong>Polything</strong>, we believe that r<a href="https://polything.co.uk/services/" >esponsible marketing </a>is not just about promoting products and services—it&#8217;s about fostering a sustainable future for our planet. As a marketing consultancy, we are acutely aware of the environmental impacts associated with our industry. That&#8217;s why we are committed to mitigating these effects and raising awareness among our clients about what sustainable growth truly means in marketing.</p> <h2><strong>Our Commitment to Environmental Sustainability</strong></h2> <ul> <li><strong>Current Footprint</strong>: Our estimated annual carbon footprint is <strong>1.67 tonnes of CO₂e per employee</strong>, totalling <strong>3.33 tonnes</strong> for our entire company. This is <strong>60% lower than the industry average</strong>, but we recognise there&#8217;s more work to be done. For information on how this has been calculated please visit <a href="https://action.small99.co.uk/progress/polything-ltd" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Small99</a>.</li> <li><strong>Actions Taken</strong>: <ul> <li><strong>Switched to Renewable Energy</strong>: We have moved to <strong>Ecotricity</strong> as our energy supplier, ensuring that our operations are powered by 100% renewable sources.</li> <li><strong>Implemented Energy-Efficient Lighting</strong>: We&#8217;ve conducted an audit of our lighting systems and replaced all non-LED lights with energy-efficient LEDs to reduce energy consumption.</li> <li><strong>Home Energy Initiatives</strong>: For our team members working from home, we&#8217;ve encouraged and facilitated the switch to renewable energy suppliers.</li> </ul> </li> <li><strong>Ongoing Efforts</strong>: <ul> <li><strong>Measuring Our Carbon Footprint</strong>: We&#8217;re utilising tools like <strong>Sage Earth</strong> and <strong>Clean Growth UK</strong> to accurately measure and monitor our emissions.</li> <li><strong>Supply Chain Evaluation</strong>: We&#8217;re exporting supply chain data to understand the environmental impact of our top suppliers.</li> </ul> </li> </ul> <h2><strong>Future Goals</strong></h2> <ul> <li><strong>Carbon Negative by 2030</strong>: We aim to not just offset but exceed our carbon emissions, making us carbon negative.</li> <li><strong>Continuous Improvement</strong>: We&#8217;re exploring innovative solutions for carbon reduction and investing in technologies that support our sustainability objectives.</li> </ul> <h2><strong>Social Responsibility</strong></h2> <ul> <li><strong>Client Advocacy</strong>: We&#8217;re proactively engaging with our clients to discuss their sustainability agendas, encouraging them to measure their carbon footprints and adopt renewable energy solutions.</li> <li><strong>Team Engagement</strong>: Our team is our greatest asset. We hold monthly meetings to share progress, brainstorm ideas, and ensure everyone contributes to our sustainability journey.</li> <li><strong>Community Involvement</strong>: We&#8217;re exploring opportunities for volunteering and charitable donations to support environmental causes within our community.</li> </ul> <h2><strong>Supply Chain Management</strong></h2> <ul> <li><strong>Sustainable Partnerships</strong>: We prioritise working with suppliers who share our commitment to sustainability. For instance, we use <strong>Google Workspace</strong>, which is striving for net-zero emissions and 24/7 carbon-free energy operations by 2030.</li> <li><strong>Supplier Support</strong>: We engage with our suppliers to understand their sustainability practices and offer support to align their efforts with our goals.</li> </ul> <h2><strong>Stakeholder Engagement</strong></h2> <ul> <li><strong>Open Communication</strong>: We maintain transparent communication with our employees, clients, and other stakeholders about our sustainability initiatives.</li> <li><strong>Collaborative Efforts</strong>: Feedback and collaboration are encouraged to enhance our ESG (Environmental, Social, and Governance) strategies.</li> </ul> <h2><strong>Join Us on Our Journey</strong></h2> <p>Sustainability is a collective effort. We invite our clients, partners, and community to join us in redefining sustainable growth in marketing. Together, we can make a meaningful impact on the environment while driving success for our businesses.</p> <h2><strong>Contact Us</strong></h2> <p>For more information about our sustainability initiatives or to collaborate with us on environmentally responsible marketing strategies, please <a href="https://polything.co.uk/contact/" >get in touch</a>.</p>
